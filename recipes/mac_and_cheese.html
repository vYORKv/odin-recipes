Smooth Mac and Cheese

Description

Get ready for a smooth, smothered mac and cheese recipe that won't get packed in with the leftovers! This classic side dish is sure to be the first to go!

Ingredients

  • Any mac and cheese box from your local store
  • 1 lb of cheddar cheese
  • Butter

Directions

  1. Open mac and cheese box and set aside powdered cheese sauce (we'll need this later!)
  2. Boil macaroni noodles in pot of water until soft
  3. Turn stove down until water becomes a simmer
  4. Add 1 stick of butter to the pot
  5. Let simmer for 10 minutes
  6. Add powedered cheese sauce to pot
  7. Mix well, then drain the pot
  8. Pour contents of pot into a casserole dish
  9. Preheat oven to 375°
  10. Microwave 1 stick of butter until liquid
  11. Pour stick of butter into casserole dish and stir
  12. Cover casserole dish with 1lb of cheddar cheese
  13. Bake in oven for 15 minutes
  14. Remove and serve while hot!
  15. Yum!
