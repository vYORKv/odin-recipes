Decadent Sweet Potatoe Marshmallow Casserole

Description

The perfect end to a perfect meal! This dessert dish will end the day on a high note! Just remember to make enough to have leftovers, because you'll be craving it the next day, too!

Ingredients

  • 2 sticks of butter
  • A bundle of sweet potatoes
  • A bag of marshmallows
  • Milk
  • Sugar

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 300°
  2. Boil whole sweet potatoes until tender
  3. Place boiled sweet potatoes into bowl for mixing
  4. Mash sweet potatoes
  5. Pour 1 cup of milk and 2 cups of sugar into mash bowl
  6. Continue to mash with milk and sugar until soft
  7. Microwave 1 stick of butter until liquid
  8. Pour butter into mash and stir
  9. Move mash from bowl to casserole dish and spread even
  10. Place marshmallows on top of mash (Get Creative! A smiley face will brighten the dish!)
  11. Place in oven to bake for 15 minutes
  12. Best served hot!
  13. If you remembered to make extra, just reheat leftovers in microwave the next day!
  14. Yum!
