Savory Thanksgiving Turkey

Description

This dish is the centerpiece of any Thanksgiving meal. A beautifully basted turkey baked to perfection. The tender meat and the crispy skin will have everyone coming back for more!

Ingredients

  • Whole Turkey
  • Chicken Broth
  • Butter
  • Salt and Pepper

Directions

How much you use of each ingredient doesn't matter. Larger portions = more flavor!

  1. Preheat the oven to 500°
  2. Place turkey in baking pan
  3. Pour chicken broth over turkey
  4. Microwave 2 sticks of butter until liquid
  5. Pour liquid butter over turkey
  6. Generously salt and pepper turkey
  7. Once oven is preheated, bake for 90 minutes
  8. Remove and let rest for 1 hour
  9. Enjoy!
